Stir Fried Beef Recipe

Ingredients list by Christine Gallary

Beef & Sauce
  • 8 ounces flank steak, trimmed of excess fat and sinew
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon toasted sesame seed oil
The Stir Fry
  • Steamed white rice or brown rice, for servings
  • 3 teaspoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 8 ounces fresh brown or porcini mushrooms, halved or quartered if large Kosher salt
  • 1 bunch asparagus (about 1 pound), ends trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 teaspoons peeled, minced fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds (optional)
